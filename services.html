Children Counseling

At Creative Development Centre, we provide a safe, supportive, and child-friendly environment where children can express their thoughts and feelings freely. Our trained counsellors work closely with children to address emotional, behavioral, and academic challenges.

Children often face a variety of challenges as they grow — from academic pressure and peer conflicts to family changes and emotional struggles. Our children counselling services are designed to help young individuals navigate these challenges with confidence, resilience, and clarity.

We use age-appropriate techniques including play therapy, art therapy, and structured conversations to help children express themselves and develop healthy coping mechanisms. Our counsellors work in close collaboration with parents and teachers to ensure a holistic support system for each child.

Key Benefits of Children Counseling:

  • Helps children manage emotions like anger, sadness, and anxiety
  • Improves academic performance and concentration
  • Builds self-esteem and social skills
  • Addresses behavioral issues in a compassionate way
  • Supports children through family transitions like divorce or relocation
  • Develops healthy communication and problem-solving skills

Who Can Benefit?

Our children counselling services are suitable for children aged 4–17 years dealing with school refusal, bullying, learning difficulties, social withdrawal, mood changes, trauma, or any other emotional concerns. Early intervention makes a significant difference in a child's long-term mental health.

Ready to support your child's emotional well-being? Book a session today.

Marriage Counseling

At the Creative Development Centre, we provide supportive and confidential Marriage Counseling services to help couples strengthen their bond. Whether you're facing communication issues, emotional disconnect, or recurring conflicts, our expert counselors guide couples in understanding each other better.

Our approach helps both partners express their thoughts openly and respectfully in a safe space. Our approach to Marriage Counseling is tailored to each couple's unique needs. By improving emotional connection, conflict resolution skills, and mutual understanding, we aim to restore harmony and intimacy in your relationship.

Marriage counseling is not just for relationships in crisis — it's also a proactive step toward a stronger, healthier future together.

Key Benefits of Marriage Counseling:

  • Improves communication and emotional understanding
  • Helps resolve ongoing conflicts and misunderstandings
  • Rebuilds trust and strengthens emotional connection
  • Supports couples during life changes or transitions
  • Provides tools for long-term relationship health
  • Creates a safe, neutral space to discuss sensitive issues

Our Approach:

We use evidence-based methods including Emotionally Focused Therapy (EFT), Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), and the Gottman Method to help couples build stronger, healthier relationships. Sessions are fully confidential and judgement-free.

Senior Citizen Counseling

Growing older brings unique life changes — retirement, health challenges, loss of loved ones, and shifting family dynamics. Our Senior Citizen Counseling services provide compassionate emotional support to help elderly individuals navigate these transitions with dignity and peace of mind.

At Creative Development Centre, we understand that senior citizens often face feelings of loneliness, purposelessness, and anxiety about the future. Our counsellors create a warm, respectful environment where elders can openly share their concerns and receive personalized guidance.

We also offer home visit counselling for seniors who have mobility constraints, ensuring that professional support is always accessible regardless of physical limitations.

Key Benefits of Senior Citizen Counseling:

  • Addresses loneliness, isolation, and feelings of neglect
  • Helps cope with retirement and loss of purpose
  • Supports grieving over loss of spouse or peers
  • Manages anxiety about health conditions and mortality
  • Improves family relationships and communication
  • Home visits available for those with mobility issues

We care for your elders. Book a session or request a home visit today.

Personal Counseling

Personal counselling is a one-on-one therapeutic process that focuses on you — your thoughts, feelings, goals, and challenges. At Creative Development Centre, we offer a safe, non-judgmental space where you can explore your inner self and work towards positive personal growth.

Whether you're dealing with low self-esteem, life transitions, career confusion, grief, or simply feeling stuck — personal counselling provides the clarity and emotional support you need to move forward. Our counsellors help you identify patterns, challenge limiting beliefs, and develop actionable strategies for a more fulfilling life.

Key Benefits of Personal Counseling:

  • Helps overcome personal obstacles and limiting beliefs
  • Provides clarity in career, relationships, and life decisions
  • Supports through life transitions like job change, relocation, or loss
  • Develops healthy coping strategies for everyday stress
  • Improves overall mental and emotional well-being

Financial Counseling

Money-related stress is one of the most common yet least talked-about sources of anxiety. At Creative Development Centre, our Financial Counseling services help individuals and families manage financial stress, develop healthy money habits, and make informed financial decisions.

Our counsellors provide a supportive, non-judgmental environment where you can openly discuss your financial concerns — from debt and budgeting to financial conflicts in relationships. We help you create practical plans to improve your financial health while also addressing the emotional aspects of financial stress.

Key Benefits of Financial Counseling:

  • Reduces financial stress and anxiety
  • Helps create and stick to a realistic budget
  • Provides guidance on managing debt effectively
  • Addresses financial conflicts between partners or family members
  • Supports better financial decision-making
  • Builds long-term financial confidence and literacy

Stress and Anxiety Management

In today's fast-paced world, stress and anxiety have become increasingly common. At Creative Development Centre, we help individuals identify the root causes of their stress and anxiety, and equip them with practical tools to restore emotional balance and mental clarity.

Unmanaged stress and anxiety can affect every area of your life — work, relationships, health, and daily functioning. Our counsellors use evidence-based techniques including mindfulness, relaxation training, and cognitive restructuring to help you break the cycle of stress and build long-term resilience.

Key Benefits of Stress & Anxiety Management:

  • Identifies personal stress triggers and patterns
  • Teaches effective relaxation and breathing techniques
  • Reduces physical symptoms of stress (insomnia, headaches)
  • Improves focus, productivity, and daily functioning
  • Builds long-term emotional resilience
  • Provides tools to prevent anxiety escalation

Techniques We Use:

Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction (MBSR), Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT), Progressive Muscle Relaxation, Breathing exercises, and Journaling techniques — all customized to your individual needs.

Reclaim your peace of mind. Start your stress management journey today.

Depression Support

Depression is more than just feeling sad — it's a serious condition that affects how you think, feel, and function in daily life. At Creative Development Centre, we offer compassionate, professional support to help individuals understand and manage their depression in a safe, confidential environment.

Our experienced counsellors work with individuals experiencing mild to moderate depression, helping them identify negative thought patterns, develop coping strategies, and gradually rebuild their sense of purpose and joy. We believe that with the right support, recovery is possible for everyone.

Key Benefits of Depression Support:

  • Provides a safe space to openly discuss feelings without judgment
  • Helps identify and challenge negative thought patterns
  • Develops personalized coping strategies for daily challenges
  • Improves motivation, sleep, and energy levels
  • Rebuilds self-worth and sense of purpose
  • Supports gradual return to meaningful activities

You Are Not Alone:

Depression is highly treatable. Taking the first step to seek help is the most important decision you can make. Our counsellors are trained to provide empathetic, evidence-based support — and we walk this journey with you, one step at a time.

You don't have to face depression alone. Reach out to us today.
